  • In Search of an Understandable Consensus Algorithm (Raft)

    Authors: Diego Ongaro and John Ousterhout

    Raft is a consensus algorithm designed as an alternative to Paxos. It was meant to be easier to understand than Paxos by decomposing the consensus problem into leader election, log replication, and safety.
